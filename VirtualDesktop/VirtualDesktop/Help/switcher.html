The Switcher

The switcher is a full screen display that shows all of your open windows on every virtual desktop. You can access the switcher by pressing Windows Key + Z by default. The switcher includes the following features.

  • View live previews of your open windows.
  • Drag windows between desktops.
  • Arrange windows on a desktop.
  • Move windows in real time.
  • Configurable fade in/out.
  • Configurable background color.
  • Double-click a window to switch to that desktop and activate that window.
  • Double-click a blank space on a desktop square to switch to that desktop.
