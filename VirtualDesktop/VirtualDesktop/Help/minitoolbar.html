Mini-Toolbar

The mini-toolbar bar is a very lean tool designed to be unobtrusive and simple to use. Here are its basic features:

  • Move the toolbar by dragging its handle
  • Change the toolbar between vertical and horizontal alignment by double-clicking the handle
  • Switch to a desktop by single-clicking it
  • The toolbar will snap the edges of your monitor(s)
  • Right-clicking a desktop icon will give you a list of commands pertaining to it
